❌ I bought many equipments.
✅ I bought a lot of equipment.
Explanation: The word "equipment" is uncountable in English. It never takes an "s" at the end.
❌ The roller coaster was very funny.
✅ The roller coaster was a lot of fun.
Explanation: "Fun" means enjoyable. "Funny" means something makes you laugh (like a clown or a joke).
❌ He runs very fastly.
✅ He runs very fast.
Explanation: There is no word "fastly" in English. "Fast" is used as both an adjective and an adverb.
❌ I am happy from my new shoes.
✅ I am happy with my new shoes.
Explanation: In English, we use the preposition "with" or "about" after happy, not "from".
❌ She spreaded the butter.
✅ She spread the butter.
Explanation: "Spread" is an irregular verb. The past tense is still "spread", never "spreaded".
